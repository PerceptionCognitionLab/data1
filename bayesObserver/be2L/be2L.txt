sub: subject number
fast: Too fast or not (0=fine, 1= too fast)
i: trial number
b: block number
j: trial w/in a block
ecc: eccentricity condition (1 easiest, 4 hardest)
eccVal: eccentricity value (.2,.88,.95,1)
theta: orientation degree of ellipse (in radians, w.r.t vertical, clockwise)
resp: subjects response in radians
rt: response time
trialPts: points earned on trial
totPts: sum of points earned
